The Core Concepts

The Rand ($R$)

A unit of transmission fidelity. It measures the delta between two sources. $1.0$ Rand represents lossless transmission. $0$ Rands represents total entropic noise where the signal is obliterated.

The Prototaxis

The smallest discrete packet of semantic information. To count as 1 Prototaxis, a statement must establish a Subject-Predicate-Referent chain verifiable as a physical interaction. If it's a value judgment (e.g., "The vibes are bad"), it has 0 prototaxis.

The Semantic Event Horizon

Typically occurring at 0.05 Rands. This is the "Point of No Return" where a medium stops reporting on an event and begins reporting on other reports. It is the realm of pure hyperreality and propaganda.
